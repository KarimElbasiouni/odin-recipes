Hummus

This hummus recipe is a simple way to prepare one of middle east's most popular dishes to enjoy with family and friends.

Ingredients:

  • 1(15 ounce) can garbanzo beans (chickpeas), drained
  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon freshlemon juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin (Optional)
  • 1 pinch paprika
  • 1 sprig fresh parsley, chopped

Steps:

  1. Place garbanzo beans, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, water, garlic and cumin into a food processor and process until smooth, about 1 minute. Transfer to a bowl and sprinkle the top of the hummus with paprika and fresh parsley.
